Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

What is Ediify Concept's Personal Doubt–Solving Service?

+
It's a WhatsApp-based academic support system where students can ask their specific subject-related or study/strategy doubts and receive clear, simplified explanations directly from the Ediify Concept Team.

Who can use this service?

+
This service is specially designed for ICSE (Classes 9–10) and ISC (Classes 11–12) students who want fast, reliable, and concept-focused academic guidance.

How do I ask my question?

+
Click the "Ask Your Doubt Now" button on this page — it will open our official WhatsApp Business account. Send your specific doubt as a message (one concept or question at a time).

How much does it cost?

+
Each question costs ₹29, payable directly via UPI on WhatsApp. This small one-time fee covers a detailed, personally written solution for that particular doubt.

How long will it take to get my reply?

+
You'll receive your solution within 1 hour (during working hours). For questions sent late at night, the reply will be provided the next morning.

What kind of questions can I ask?

+
You can ask:

• Subject-based doubts from ICSE/ISC subjects — English, History & Civics, Geography, Political Science, Psychology, etc.
• General academic or strategy-related queries, such as study planning, time management, or answer presentation techniques.

Can I ask multiple questions?

+
Yes. Once connected on WhatsApp, you can continue asking new questions anytime. Each additional question will be charged separately at ₹29, payable via UPI in the same chat.

How will I receive my solution?

+
All answers are sent via chat on WhatsApp in simple, clear language — formatted to help you understand the concept instantly.

Who provides the answers?

+
All explanations are created by the Ediify Concept academic team, under the guidance of Jasman Anand (Founder of Ediify Concept) to ensure accuracy and conceptual clarity.

What if I face any issue or don't receive my solution?

+
If you ever face a delay or technical issue, simply message again on WhatsApp or email us at edifyconcept06@gmail.com — our team will resolve it promptly.

How does this service fit into the Ediify Concept Ecosystem?

+
The Doubt-Solving Service is a part of the Ediify Concept Ecosystem.

While Publications help you build concepts and Ediify Nexus helps you plan your studies, this service ensures you never stay stuck — giving you instant clarity whenever you need it.
