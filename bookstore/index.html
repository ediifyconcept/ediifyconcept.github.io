Before You Start Exploring

Printed Books Vs E-Books

Both formats contain the same verified academic content. The difference lies in the format, pricing, and delivery method.

  • E-Books are digital PDF editions delivered instantly after purchase and are generally priced lower, as they do not involve printing, packaging, or shipping costs.
  • Printed Books are physical copies shipped to your address after ordering and include printing and delivery costs in the price.

Please note: Some categories (such as Project Samples) may be available only in E-Book format.

About Multi-Part Books

Some syllabus-based publications with larger syllabi may be divided into multiple parts (e.g., Part I & Part II) to improve readability, revision efficiency, and the overall learning experience. This applies to both Printed Books and E-Books. Where applicable, all parts together provide complete coverage of the syllabus.

About Our Bundles

Some publications are available as Bundles, combining multiple Printed Books OR E-Books at a reduced total cost. Example: The ICSE Class 10 English E–Books (Language + Literature) Bundle costs less than purchasing the two subjects separately.

Understanding Our Publications

Ediify Concept offers three types of publications, each designed for a specific purpose in your preparation:

Syllabus-Based Publications

Complete and simplified coverage of the prescribed ICSE/ISC syllabus — designed for concept clarity and exam readiness.

Project Samples

Well-structured sample projects to guide you in creating your own unique, high-quality submissions.

Topper Strategy Publications

Proven, all-year study strategies, planning methods, and execution frameworks designed to help you achieve 95%+ scores.

What Makes Our Syllabus-Based Publications Different?

The following features apply primarily to our syllabus-based publications. Certain features may vary depending on the subject and publication type.

Simplified Language

Complex concepts are explained in a clear, easy-to-understand language, making learning easier, faster, and less stressful compared to conventional textbooks.

Complete Syllabus Coverage

Each book is designed to cover the entire prescribed ICSE/ISC syllabus in a single resource, eliminating the need to refer to multiple books for exam preparation.

No Unnecessary Content

Unlike traditional textbooks that often include extensive out-of-syllabus material, our books focus only on what students actually need to study for their examinations.

Real-Life Practical Examples

Concepts are supported with practical, real-world examples to strengthen understanding and help students confidently tackle application-based and competency-based questions.

Built-In Revision Tools

Every book is designed to simplify revision through features such as summary tables, mnemonic devices, comparison charts, and other quick-reference resources that help students revise efficiently before examinations.

Subject-Specific Revision Resources

Each publication includes specialized revision aids tailored to its subject. Depending on the publication, these may include formula sheets, important constitutional articles, historical contributions, chronological sheets, and other exam-oriented resources designed for faster recall and better retention.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

What kind of books does Ediify Concept publish?

+
Ediify Concept publishes simplified, exam-focused academic books for ICSE (Class 9 & 10) and ISC (Class 11 & 12). Our collection includes syllabus-based study books, topper strategy books for higher scores, and ISC Class 12 project sample books for unique and well-structured project preparation. Each resource is created to simplify learning and improve exam performance.

Are your books available in both printed and e-book formats?

+
Our syllabus-based books and topper strategy books are available in both formats — Printed and E-Book. Project Sample books, however, are currently available only in E-Book format. All available formats can be explored directly on this page.

How can I buy these books?

+

Each publication listed on this page includes a "View Details" button that provides complete information about the resource.

You may either purchase a publication directly using the purchase button provided within the publication details, or add multiple publications to your cart and proceed to checkout.

In both cases, WhatsApp will open with a pre-filled message containing your selected publication(s), allowing you to contact us directly and complete your purchase.

Which format should I choose — Printed or E-Book?

+
Choose the E-Book for instant access and easy portability, or the Printed Book if you prefer physical study material. Both contain the same verified content.

Why are E–Books priced significantly lower than Printed Books?

+
E-Books do not involve printing, packaging, or shipping costs, allowing us to offer them at a significantly lower price. Both Printed Books and E-Books contain the same verified academic content; the difference lies only in the format and delivery method.

Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available for printed books?

+
Currently, printed books are available on a prepaid basis only. As all printed titles are produced through on-demand printing, Cash on Delivery is not available at the moment.

In how much time would printed books be delivered?

+
Printed books are delivered within 10–15 days from the date of order, as all printed books are produced through on-demand printing. For instant access without any waiting period, students may opt for the E-Book format.

Can I trust the content quality of these books?

+
Absolutely. All titles are authored by our Founder, Jasman Anand (AIR 9 – ISC 2025, AIR 19 – ICSE 2023), and are based on real exam trends. Thousands of students have improved their scores using these resources.

Do you release updated editions?

+
Yes. Updated editions are released primarily for our syllabus-based books each academic year to reflect the latest syllabus, exam trends, and board guidelines. Updates for other categories may be released whenever necessary. You can stay informed by following our official YouTube channel and Telegram updates.

How can I get support after purchase?

+
You can reach us anytime at ediifyconcept@gmail.com for any query or assistance regarding your order or study material.

How do these books fit into the Ediify Concept Ecosystem?

+
Ediify Concept books are a core part of the Ediify Concept Ecosystem — a structured academic approach to learning. They help you build concepts, while tools like Ediify Nexus support planning, and the Doubt-Solving Service helps you resolve challenges instantly. Together, these components create a smooth and effective learning process throughout the academic year.
