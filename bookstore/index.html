Before You Start Exploring

Printed Books vs E-Books

Both contain the same content. The only difference lies in format — E-Books are digital PDFs delivered instantly upon purchase, while Printed Books are shipped to your physical address after ordering.

Please note: Some categories (such as Project Samples) may be available only in E-Book format.

About Our Bundles

Some publications are available as Bundles, combining multiple Printed Books OR E-Books at a reduced total cost. Example: The ICSE Class 10 English Digital Notes (Language + Literature) Bundle costs less than purchasing the two subjects separately.

Understanding Our Publications

Ediify Concept offers three types of publications, each designed for a specific purpose in your preparation:

Syllabus-Based Publications

Complete and simplified coverage of the prescribed ICSE/ISC syllabus — designed for concept clarity and exam readiness.

Project Samples

Well-structured sample projects to guide you in creating your own unique, high-quality submissions.

Topper Strategy Publications

Proven, all-year study strategies, planning methods, and execution frameworks designed to help you achieve 95%+ scores.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

What kind of books does Ediify Concept publish?

+
Ediify Concept publishes simplified, exam-focused academic books for ICSE (Class 9 & 10) and ISC (Class 11 & 12). Our collection includes syllabus-based study books, topper strategy books for higher scores, and ISC Class 12 project sample books for unique and well-structured project preparation. Each resource is created to simplify learning and improve exam performance.

Are your books available in both printed and e-book formats?

+
Our syllabus-based books and topper strategy books are available in both formats — Printed and E-Book. Project Sample books, however, are currently available only in E-Book format. All available formats can be explored directly on this page.

Where can I buy these books?

+
All printed books and all e-books are available through our WhatsApp catalogues via the buttons provided on this page.

Which format should I choose — Printed or E-Book?

+
Choose the E-Book for instant access and easy portability, or the Printed Book if you prefer physical study material. Both contain the same verified content.

Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?

+
Currently, printed books are available on a prepaid basis only. As all printed titles are produced through on-demand printing, Cash on Delivery is not available at the moment.

In how much time would printed books be delivered?

+
Printed books are delivered within 10–15 days from the date of order, as all printed books are produced through on-demand printing. For instant access without any waiting period, students may opt for the E-Book format.

Can I trust the content quality of these books?

+
Absolutely. All titles are authored by our Founder, Jasman Anand (AIR 9 – ISC 2025, AIR 19 – ICSE 2023), and are based on real exam trends. Thousands of students have improved their scores using these resources.

Do you release updated editions?

+
Yes. Updated editions are released primarily for our syllabus-based books each academic year to reflect the latest syllabus, exam trends, and board guidelines. Updates for other categories may be released whenever necessary. You can stay informed by following our official YouTube channel and Telegram updates.

How can I get support after purchase?

+
You can reach us anytime at edifyconcept06@gmail.com for any query or assistance regarding your order or study material.

How do these books fit into the Ediify Concept Ecosystem?

+
Ediify Concept books are a core part of the Ediify Concept Ecosystem — a structured academic approach to learning. They help you build concepts, while tools like Ediify Nexus support planning, and the Doubt-Solving Service helps you resolve challenges instantly. Together, these components create a smooth and effective learning process throughout the academic year.
