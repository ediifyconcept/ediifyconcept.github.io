About Ediify Concept

Founded in 2023 by Jasman Anand, Ediify Concept is an educational platform dedicated to simplifying ICSE Class 10 and ISC Class 12 learning. From expertly crafted academic books and project samples to proven exam strategies, our mission is to make preparation stress-free and result-oriented.

We've already helped thousands of students across India through our books, videos, and resources — ensuring clarity, confidence, and top scores.

Mission: To make board prep smarter, not harder.

Motto: "Har doubt ka the end — Ediify is your real friend."

Founder's Vision

Jasman Anand – Founder of Ediify Concept

Meet Jasman Anand — an educator, author, and academic achiever driven by a single mission: to make learning simpler, smarter, and more accessible for every student.

Having personally experienced the challenges of ICSE and ISC preparation, Jasman founded Ediify Concept with a clear vision — to simplify complex concepts, remove academic overwhelm, and empower students to reach their full potential with confidence.

He believes that education shouldn't feel like a burden — it should be a bridge to discovery, clarity, and excellence.

Through his publications, e-books, and digital learning innovations, Jasman aims to transform the future of learning by making simplified, high-quality education accessible to every student across India and beyond.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

1. What is Ediify Concept?

+
Ediify Concept is an educational platform founded in 2023 by Jasman Anand to simplify ICSE and ISC learning. We provide exam-focused publications, smart digital tools, and personalized support to help students score higher with less stress.

2. What kind of resources does Ediify Concept offer?

+
We offer three core categories:

📗 Publications – Printed & E-Books for ICSE and ISC students
🔢 Ediify Nexus – Smart calculators, planners, and trackers for efficient study management
💬 Doubt–Solving Service – Personalized academic guidance for ICSE & ISC students via WhatsApp

+
